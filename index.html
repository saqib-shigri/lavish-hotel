About Us

Nestled in the heart of Skardu Valley, The Lavish Hotel blends modern luxury with authentic Balti hospitality. Overlooking the mighty Karakoram Range, we offer world-class amenities, curated experiences, and unforgettable mountain views.

Since our founding, we have been committed to providing exceptional service, luxurious accommodations, and a gateway to the breathtaking landscapes of Gilgit-Baltistan.
